Workshop Assistant

An AI helper for local workshop hosts who need to turn a rough idea into a clear first launch plan.

Example map generated from a short product brief. Static local HTML, no external assets.

Who It Is For

known

Primary user

People who run local workshops and are comfortable using AI chat tools, but do not want technical setup.

known

Problem

They have an idea, but need help turning it into a clear landing page outline and launch checklist.

assumption

Success

A host can explain the workshop, list what is needed, and test the idea with three real people.

Product Blocks

known

Idea intake

Collects the workshop topic, audience, format, promise, and constraints in plain language.

known

Landing outline

Turns the idea into headline, audience, offer, agenda, proof, FAQ, and call to action.

known

Launch checklist

Lists the minimum tasks before sharing the workshop publicly.

unknown

Payments and signup

Login, payment, database, and email flow are not decided yet.

User Flow

  1. User describes the workshop idea in normal words.
  2. Assistant asks only the missing questions needed for a first version.
  3. Assistant creates a landing page outline.
  4. Assistant creates a practical launch checklist.
  5. User tests the outline with three workshop hosts or potential attendees.

Risks And Open Questions

  • risk: Building payments, login, or database too early may slow down the first test.
  • unknown: Who is the first workshop niche: fitness, cooking, education, business, art?
  • unknown: Does the user need a website, a PDF, or just a shareable text outline for the first test?
  • risk: Do not paste private attendee data or payment credentials into an AI agent.
